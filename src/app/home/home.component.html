The Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) is Asia's oldest stock exchange. Based in Mumbai, India, BSE was established in 1875 as the Native Share & Stock Brokers' Association. Prior to that brokers and traders would gather under banyan trees to conduct transactions.
The NSE was established in 1994 as the first materialised electronic exchange in the country. NSE was the first exchange in the country to provide a modern, fully automated screen-based electronic trading system which offered easy trading facility to the investors spread across the length and breadth of the country.
